Dress Code

Please dress in traditional wedding attire — suits, gowns, cocktail dresses, and formal separates are all welcome. The following are the San Gabriel Country Club's dress code requirements for all guests.

Men

  • Shirts must be tucked in unless straight-cut or designed to be worn untucked. Mock turtleneck collars are accepted.
  • Shorts must be no shorter than 4″ above the center of the back of the knee.
  • Hats and visors must be removed while inside any area of the Clubhouse. They are permitted outdoors (grill patio, lounge patio) and must be worn bill-forward.
  • No hoodies of any kind are permitted inside the Clubhouse, except in the pro shop, locker room, Men's Cardroom, Flagstone Patio, and Lounge Patio — and must be golf-specific, subject to Head Professional review.

Women

  • Dresses, blouses, skirts, tailored slacks, and Bermuda shorts are all welcome.
  • Shorts and skirts must be no shorter than fingertip length at rest.
  • Hats are permitted throughout the Clubhouse, provided they are not a cap or visor.
  • No hoodies of any kind are permitted inside the Clubhouse, except in the pro shop, locker room, Women's Cardroom, Flagstone Patio, and Lounge Patio — and must be golf-specific, subject to Head Professional review.
